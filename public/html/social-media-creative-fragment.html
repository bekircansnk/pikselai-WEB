Social media design is a dynamic and visually engaging aspect of online communication that revolves around the creation of tailored images and graphics specifically crafted for different social media platforms. It is a creative endeavor where designers strategically fuse their skills with an understanding of the platform's context, audience and purpose. Social media design is about capturing attention and conveying messages effectively within the constraints of a digital space. Not only static images, social media design also extends to dynamic like video, motion, 3D effects and AR overlays that add an extra layer of appeal and immersiveness.
